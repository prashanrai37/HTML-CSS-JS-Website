Measurements Of Dispersion

In statistics, we collect observations or measurements of some variables. These observations are known as data. Variables associated with non-numerical data are qualitative variables, and variables associated with numerical data are quantitative variables.

The study of spread of data is continusly used throghtout computer science. One of the most popular uses in current times is data analysis done by large companies.
